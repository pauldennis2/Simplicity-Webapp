On this screen you will see a list of your existing ships and have the ability to create new ships provided you have enough production in your pool. (See planets help for an explanation of production). Here is a basic overview of how combat ships work:

Generator: every ship has a generator that has two characteristics - power per turn and reserve power. Reserve power is like your "battery" or "bank" of power.
Shield: a defensive barrier that reduces damage to the ship. Shields regenerate fully outside of combat and can regenerate (slowly) in combat. Shields consume power equal to the damage blocked, which is based on the size of the ship (bigger ships have more powerful shields).
