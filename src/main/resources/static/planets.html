Planets View Help
There is not anything to "do" right now in this view. It's simply a list of the planets that are a part of your empire, with some information about them. We'll go over this information and the general mechanics of planets on this page.

Properties of Planets

  • Name - right now planets are named for their system and can't be changed.
  • Size - the maximum population the planet can support (a static number). Randomly generated, range of about 9 to 12.
  • Population - the current population of the planet.
  • Owner - expressed through the icon next to the planet: (Does not show on this screen since all these planets are yours)
  • Image - No effect on gameplay.
  • Turns to Growth - planets with room to grow (population < size) grow at 5% per turn.
  • Output Priorities - (see below)

Output Priorities

Each planet divides its output as you see fit between Production - which goes to your Production Pool and is in turn used to purchase ships - and Research - which does to your Research Pool which sadly does nothing at the present time.

How it works: You decide in percentage the amount to assign to each output. For example, you could do 50/50, 80/20, etc. These percentages are then multiplied by the population to determine the output. A planet with 10 population that is assigned 80% production and 20% research would produce 8 production and 2 research each turn.
